Arijit Singh

Singer, Composer

Date of Birth: April 25, 1987

Arijit Singh is one of India's most soulful and versatile playback singers. Known for his mesmerizing voice, he has delivered numerous chartbusters in Bollywood, from romantic ballads to energetic anthems. His hits like Tum Hi Ho, Channa Mereya, and Kesariya have won millions of hearts worldwide.

Martin Garrix

DJ, Music Producer

Date of Birth: 3May 14, 1996

Martin Garrix is a globally renowned Dutch DJ and music producer, best known for hits like Animals, Scared to Be Lonely, and High on Life. He has headlined major music festivals like Tomorrowland and Ultra Music Festival, shaping the modern electronic dance music (EDM) scene with his electrifying beats.
